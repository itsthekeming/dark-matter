**Draconia** ([Draconic](/compendium/Draconic): <span className="font-draconic">Draconia</span>) is a planet located in the [Chromesus system](/compendium/Chromesus). It is the homeworld of the [Kobolds](/compendium/Kobold). Draconia is the first known planet where the [eternal dragons](/compendium/Eternal_Dragon) seeded [dragons](/compendium/Dragon).
