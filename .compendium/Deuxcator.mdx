**Deuxcator** ([Elvish](/compendium/Elvish): <span className="font-elvish">Deuxcator</span>) is the third planet in the [Etilion system](/compendium/Etilion). It is the home of the elven [Great House](/compendium/Great_House) [Mala’Fiil](/compendium/Mala’Fiil) and the administrative seat of the [Elven Empire](/compendium/Elven_Empire). Deuxcator also houses the [Elven War College](/compendium/Elven_War_College) and is the center of the Empire's military apparatus.
