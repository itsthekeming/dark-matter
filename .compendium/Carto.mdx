**Carto** ([Elvish](/compendium/Elvish): <span className="font-elvish">Carto</span>) is the sixth planet in the [Etilion system](/compendium/Etilion). It is the home of the elven [Great House](/compendium/Great_House) [Mere'gaal](/compendium/Mere'gaal) and a hub of elven academia and magical studies.

Carto directly intersects with a [galactic ley line](/compendium/Galactic_ley_line), greatly enhancing the magical properties of the planet and the magical abilities of its inhabitants.
